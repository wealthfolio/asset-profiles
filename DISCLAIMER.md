## Source acknowledgments

Per-record `provenance` blocks identify the upstream source. Primary
sources include:

- [JerBouma/FinanceDatabase](https://github.com/JerBouma/FinanceDatabase) — stock metadata (MIT)
- [SEC EDGAR](https://www.sec.gov/edgar) — US ETF holdings via N-PORT (US public domain)
- Issuer-published portfolio holdings files (iShares, Vanguard, State Street SPDR, Invesco, et al.) — used as fallback for non-US ETFs, with each issuer credited per record.
